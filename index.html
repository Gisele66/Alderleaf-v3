About Chris Poschmann

Alderleaf Land and Tree Services is a family owned and operated business serving Haida Gwaii since 2021. Chris has been a BC Certified Faller for 25+ years with over 22+ years of experience as a professional arborist and tree-climber.

Chris has been working with trees on Haida Gwaii since 2004 and has lived in communities including Daajing Giids, Tlell, Sandspit and Port Clements.
