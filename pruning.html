Hedges & Shrubs

A well-manicured yard is a sign of prestige in our culture. We have experience trimming hedges and shrubs of varying sizes.

Request a Free Quote

Hedges and shrubs are ornamental features often found on a residential property. Sometimes natural plants can form a hedge, a local example being salmon berry. Hedges and shrubs can be manicured into shapes and given sharp lines, or trimmed for simple size-reduction for many of the same reasons one would prune trees. Commonly, hedges and shrubs require annual or semi-annual pruning to keep them healthy and flowering well, and, occasionally, taming an overgrown shrub could require several visits over subsequent seasons. Some specialized knowledge and equipment is necessary for certain varieties of hedges and shrubs.

Pruning for view enhancement, tree and shrub health or production and also for hazard reduction.

Commitment to Safety

Chris and his team are highly skilled in working in confined spaces, and have specialized equipment and training to ensure the safety of buildings, infrastructure, surrounding vegetation and personnel. We can provide a complete cleanup of debris, and transportation and disposal of stems off-site, if desired.

Fully insured and committed to safety, you can enjoy peace-of-mind when Alderleaf is on the job.
